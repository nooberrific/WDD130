Overview

Purpose

Have you ever been mindlessly scrolling on your device, completely bored out of your mind? Well, my hope is that my website will help to solve this! Under the alias “Mickerrific,” my goal is to help rid the world of boredom and sorrow. The biggest difference between most content and mine is that I will always provide clean humor which is targeted towards all audiences. This website will help others to get to know me a little better and find some entertainment that is easily accessible on-site as well. With convenient links to my YouTube videos, as well as scripts and personal stories which aren't accessible anywhere else, the fun will be limitless. No more boredom, just quick and easy access to eventual hours of fun.

Audience

I would say that my target audience is mostly the high schoolers, middle schoolers, and young adults in the world who like to play video games, but my goal is for my website to eventually (probably gonna have to keep working on it after this classw) have a wide variety of content within it so that it can reach out to people of all ages. I especially want people who are very bored to come to my website. I want my website to be a fun place to be.
