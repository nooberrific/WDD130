How it Feels to Chew 5 Gum

I held the brittle stick in my hand, its heft surprising to the mind. An antiquity to be sure, but were the rumors true? Deducing there was but one way to find out (I was always a clever lad mind you), I took a bite and began to chew, its minty nectar gently caressing each individual ridge of my taste buds. My mind went blank. When I came to, I cried out, 'Huzzah! What lofty giddiness is inspired within my mind, evidenced in the increasing rate of palpation within my breast! Never before have I experienced such vigor coursing through my veins and even in my very being. My senses have been stimulated, nary to return to previous levels of sorrow. Truly, my friends, this is indeed, 'how it feels to chew 5 gum.'
